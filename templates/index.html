For Drivers

Where is the probability of a pick-up the highest?

Using data from pick-ups in 2015 we implemented a DBScan clustering algorithm, grouping by the Borough, as well as the date, since a disproportionate number of pick-ups occur in Manhattan, approximately 82%. We also used different clustering criteria for Manhattan since drop-offs occur within closer proximity of each other.
